About Us

At Skyline Moving, we're two motivated college students with a combined 6 years of hands-on moving experience. What started as a side hustle quickly grew into a full-time commitment to providing affordable, reliable, and high-quality moving services across Omaha, Lincoln, and the surrounding areas.

We know how stressful moving can be, so our goal is simple: make it easy, quick, and worry-free for you. We work efficiently, we're respectful of your time, and—most importantly—we handle your furniture and belongings with the same care we'd give our own.

Whether you're moving across town or just need help loading and unloading, we're here to help—at prices that won't break the bank.
